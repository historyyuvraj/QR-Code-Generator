01 Overview

QR Code Generator is a free, open-source, single-file web application created by HistoryYuvraj for designing and exporting styled QR codes. It runs entirely in your browser — nothing you type or upload is sent to a server.

05 Acceptable use

QR codes are just data containers — this tool doesn't know or judge what you encode. That said, please don't use it to:

  • Generate codes for phishing links, malware distribution, or other deceptive/malicious destinations;
  • Impersonate another person, brand, or organization without authorization;
  • Encode content that is illegal in your jurisdiction.

This is guidance, not a monitored or enforced policy — the app has no backend and no way to see what you generate. Use good judgment.

06 Privacy

QR Code Generator has no server component. Everything — your inputs, your history, your favorites, your logo uploads, your camera feed for scanning — stays in your browser's local storage and memory. Nothing is uploaded, logged, or shared with HistoryYuvraj or anyone else. Camera and clipboard access are only requested when you explicitly use the scan, copy, or paste features, and only for that purpose.
